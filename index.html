Tips for the best results

  • Include the asking price in AED
  • List all the specs you know — GPU, CPU, RAM, storage
  • Mention the platform (Dubizzle, OpenSouk, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • Add condition notes — "like new", "used 2 years", "minor scratches"
  • If it's a full build, include every component you can see
  • Seller details help — reputation, number of reviews, pickup location
The Story

About CopOrDrop.ae

Built different 💻

CopOrDrop.ae was built by a 16-year-old in Sharjah who got tired of seeing people get ripped off on Dubizzle.

You know the vibe — someone lists a GTX 1080 for 1,800 AED calling it "still powerful for gaming" and people actually buy it, not realising they're basically funding someone else's upgrade. It happens every day in every UAE tech group.

The UAE second-hand PC market is proper wild. Prices vary massively, sellers test you, and there's no easy way to know if you're getting played. So I built one.

Paste any listing. Get a verdict. Simple.

Should you trust it?

Honestly? Use it as a vibe check, not gospel. The AI knows UAE prices, understands PC specs, and has a decent nose for scams — but always do your own research too.

The price tracker is based on real listings from Dubizzle, OpenSouk, and local shops. It's not a live feed (yet), but it's way better than guessing from memory.

And if the AI is wrong? Tell us. We're literally learning from every listing.
